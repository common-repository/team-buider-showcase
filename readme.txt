== Description ==
The perfect solution to effortlessly showcase your team members on your website! This powerful plugin allows you to display your team's profiles with ease, using attractive grid and slider layouts. With a user-friendly shortcode, highlighting your team has never been simpler. Enhance your website with our Team WordPress Plugin and let your audience get to know your team with ease. == Installation ==

= Download & Upload: =
1. Download the **Team Builder Showcase** plugin (*.zip file*)
2. In your admin area, go to the Plugins menu and click on **Add New**
3. Click on **Upload Plugin** and choose the **`team-builder-showcase.zip`** file and click on **Install Now**
4. Activate the plugin and Enjoy!

= Manually: =
1. Download and upload the **Team Builder Showcase** plugin to the **`/wp-content/plugins/`** directory
2. Activate the plugin through the Plugins menu in WordPress
